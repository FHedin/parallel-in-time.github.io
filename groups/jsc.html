Jülich Supercomputing Centre

The research group at the Jülich Supercomputing Centre is lead by Dr. Robert Speck mentoring his two PhD students Dieter Moser and Torbjörn Klatt as well as currently two master students.

They concentrate on multi-level methods such as MLSDC, PFASST and Multigrid.

Their flagship is PyPinT.
