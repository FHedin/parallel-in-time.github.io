Multigrid Reduction in Time (MGRIT)

MGRIT

The algorithm by Falgout,Friedhoff,Kolev,MacLachlan and Schroder enables a scalable and non-intrusive parallel-in-time approach by applying multigrid reduction to the time dimension. See http://www.llnl.gov/casc/xbraid for more information and a place to download the XBraid software.
