Revisionsit Integral Deferred Correction (RIDC)

RIDC

The algorithm by [1] enables a small-scale, parallel-in-time approach by applying pipeline parallel deferred correction framework. See http://mathgeek.us/software.html for more information and to download the RIDC library.
